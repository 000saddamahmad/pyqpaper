Frequently Asked Questions

Are these GATE Computer Science question papers free?

Yes, all GATE Computer Science previous year question papers available on PYQpaper.in are completely free for exam preparation.

Is practicing PYQs enough for GATE CSE preparation?

PYQs help in understanding exam trends, but candidates should also revise concepts, solve mock tests, and follow standard reference books.

What is the exam mode of GATE?

GATE is a computer-based test (CBT) consisting of General Aptitude, Engineering Mathematics, and Computer Science core subjects.
