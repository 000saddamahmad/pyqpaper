CBSE Class 10 Exam Pattern

The CBSE Class 10 board exam consists of 80 marks theory and 20 marks internal assessment. Students must score at least 33% to pass the examination. The duration of the exam is 3 hours and there is no negative marking.

How to Prepare Using Previous Year Papers?

  • Complete NCERT syllabus first
  • Solve papers in a real 3-hour time limit
  • Check answers using marking scheme
  • Revise weak chapters
  • Repeat practice for best performance
