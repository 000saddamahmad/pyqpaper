UP Board Class 10 Exam Pattern

UP Board High School exam is conducted by UPMSP. The theory paper is usually of 70 marks and internal/practical carries 30 marks (subject to board guidelines). Students must score minimum passing marks to clear the examination.

How to Prepare Using UP Board Previous Year Papers?

  • Complete the full syllabus first
  • Solve papers in a fixed time limit
  • Check answers with marking scheme
  • Revise weak topics regularly
  • Practice multiple papers for best performance
