Frequently Asked Questions

Are these CDS question papers free?

Yes, all CDS previous year question papers available on PYQpaper.in are completely free for exam preparation.

Is practicing PYQs enough for CDS preparation?

PYQs help in understanding exam trends, but candidates should also revise concepts and attempt mock tests regularly.

Which subjects are included in CDS written exam?

The CDS written exam consists of: English, General Knowledge, and Elementary Mathematics (Mathematics is only for IMA, INA & AFA).
