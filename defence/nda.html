Frequently Asked Questions

Are these NDA question papers free?

Yes, all NDA previous year question papers available on PYQpaper.in are completely free for exam preparation.

Is practicing PYQs enough for NDA preparation?

PYQs help in understanding exam trends, but candidates should also revise concepts and attempt mock tests regularly.

Which subjects are included in NDA written exam?

The NDA written exam consists of two papers: Mathematics and General Ability Test (GAT).
